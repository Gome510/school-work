About Us

Student Art Coalition (SAC) is a student organization developed to support and sustain artists of color on the UCSC campus.

What SAC Does:

Educate ourselves about past and present Artists Of Color (AOC) and create art that heals but also challenges our society.

SAC's Target for Change:

To create unity between AOC on this campus and the art department itself by diversifying the current curriculum that exists in our intro courses. We wish to have our curriculum be a truer reflection of the community it teaches, which has become increasingly diverse. While our end goal is that of change, it is also equally important to us that we have a space that is conducive to the building and sustainment of community. It is vital for us to create a space in which we may heal and grow through art, our peers, and ourselves.
